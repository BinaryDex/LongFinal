Ingredients


The caffeine content of most Monster Energy drinks is approximately 10 mg/oz (33.81 mg / 100 ml), or 160 mg for a 16oz (473 ml) can. The packaging usually contains a warning label advising consumers against drinking more than 48oz per day (500 ml per day in Australia). The drinks are not recommended for pregnant women or people sensitive to caffeine. The ingredients include carbonated water, sucrose, glucose, citric acid, natural flavors, taurine, sodium citrate, color added, panax ginseng root extract, L-carnitine, L-tartrate, caffeine, sorbic acid, benzoic acid, niacinamide, sodium chloride, Glycine max glucuronolactone, inositol, guarana seed extract, pyridoxine hydrochloride, sucralose, riboflavin, maltodextrin, and cyanocobalamin.

Monster Variety

Monster Energy offers an impressive variety of flavors and types that cater to diverse tastes and preferences. From the classic Original Monster with its robust and invigorating taste to the refreshing citrusy notes of Ultra Citron and Ultra Sunrise, there's something to satisfy every palate. Berry lovers can enjoy the delicious fusion of flavors in Ultra Blue and Ultra Rosa, while those seeking a tropical escape can indulge in the exotic tastes of Pipeline Punch and Mango Loco. Additionally, Monster caters to health-conscious consumers with options like Zero Ultra and Reign Total Body Fuel, providing energy without added sugars. Whether you're craving a traditional energy boost or a refreshing twist, Monster Energy's range of flavors and types ensures there's a perfect pick-me-up for every occasion.

Sports

Monster Energy has been a prominent sponsor in various sports events and activities. From extreme sports like motocross and skateboarding to mainstream sports such as basketball and soccer, Monster Energy has established itself as a key player in the sports industry. Monster Energy has even collaborated with E-Sports players. Through sponsorships, partnerships, and events, Monster Energy continues to support athletes and enthusiasts, promoting energy and excitement in the world of sports.
